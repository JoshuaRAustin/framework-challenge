Who we are

Formed before accounting was cool, and still just as cool today, we're the ones that rocked this world 9-to-5 for the last 40 years. Remember when your boss played music over the PA and it really got you in the goove, but you didn't like how bad it sounded because of the poor quality speakers, then you thought about how much of a cheap-skate your boss really was and it caused you confront him about getting better speakers, followed by an absolutely brutal rebuttal about respect and the way the world works, and that's how you lost your second job? That was us. We're known for our bodacious performances and our stellar income reports, and as long as the world does their taxes, we'll be rocking for another 40!
